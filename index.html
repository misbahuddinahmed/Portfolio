Spill Absorb Is manufactured In The United States

manufactured In The United States

Spill Absorb is a super industrial organic absorbent made from rice husk and is a 100% biobased product. Spill Absorb will save waste management total cost as high as 50% or greater over clay products.

Spill Absorb is manufactured in the United States and has an abundant amount of renewable agriculture resources.

We are dedicated to creating high-quality, fast, effective products that will meet all your safety and facility sustainable goals.
